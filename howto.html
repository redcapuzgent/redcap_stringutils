How to use the StringUtils hook:

Limitations
  • The hook field MUST be on the same instrument as the source field.
Using the hook
Create a field with one of the following hooks:
Hook Usage
@TOLOWER={sourcefieldnamehere} Converts the field in to its lowercase equivalent.
@TOUPPER={sourcefieldnamehere} Converts the field in to its uppercase equivalent.
@STRLEN={sourcefieldnamehere} Calculated the length of a field.
@TRIM={sourcefieldnamehere} Trims the text of a field of whitespaces.
@LTRIM={sourcefieldnamehere} Trims the left side of text of a field of whitespaces.
@SUBSTR={sourcefieldnamehere1},start,end Takes a part of a string from start to end. Start and end must be valid integers.
